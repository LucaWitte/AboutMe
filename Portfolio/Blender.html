Biomolecules

These projects are a bit more useful for my professional life than modelling a donut. I started by working through the Blender for Biochemists video series by Brady Johnston. With a basic understanding of Blender and my experience from my Bachelor's thesis in Hartmut Michel's group, I grasped these concepts rather quickly. Having a good understanding of structural biology and the conventions and software used to visualize biomolecules definitely helped with the projects here!
